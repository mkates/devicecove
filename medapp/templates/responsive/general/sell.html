How Selling on VetCove Works

1Upload your listings

Add your items to the VetCove catalog using spreadsheets, our online interace, or even through a custom API

2Set your prices

Choose the prices for an anonymous buyer and a loyal buyer. Also, enter associated GPO prices of items exclusive to members of that organization

3Customers can see your products

Your products will be visible to our veterinary community

4Ship products to customers

Once you receive an order, we will pass along all the relevant shipping information (and provide a shipping label if needed)

5VetCove transfers payments to you

VetCove will wire you money or send you a check from all of your sales
