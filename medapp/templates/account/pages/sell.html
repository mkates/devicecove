Common Questions

I have many products to list. How long does it take to list a product on Vetcove?

Listing a product takes as little as two minutes. We do recommend providing detailed descriptions of the product's condition, history, and warranty to improve buyer confidence. Better quality listings may appear higher in the search results.

Are there any fees associated with selling equipment on VetCove?

For items that are listed for in person viewing, your listing is 100% free if you transact offline. If you complete the transaction online (reccommended for items that are required to be shipped), we take a standard 5% commission fee plus credit card processing fees. This charge also supports the buyer and seller protection programs.
