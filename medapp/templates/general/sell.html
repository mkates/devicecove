How Selling on VetCove Works

1Upload your listings

Add your items to the VetCove catalog using spreadsheets, our online interace, or even through a custom API

2Set your prices

Set pricing for various buyer categories and for anonymous SaveRate purchasers. We'll also work with you to ensure that GPO pricing agreements are honored and accounted for.
