Giving Back With The 1 + 1 Program

How does the 1+1 Program Work?

Every seller has the option to donate 1% of the sale of each item to one of our charities. We, in turn, also donate 1% of the sale price to the charity. A $5,000 sale results in the selected charity receiving $100! Together sellers and VetCove can make a difference in the veterinary community.

How can I participate?

Every item that you list has an option to participate in the charity. You select the charity, and when your item is sold, we will automatically deduct 1% from your payout and send your 1% + our 1% to the charity.

I want to donate to a charity that I do not see in the list.

If you don't see a charity that you think should be on VetCove, send us an email at team@vetcove.com.
