Fees and Selling

When your item sells online or a potential buyer requests to view the item offline, VetCove collects the amount paid by the buyer minus the selling and commission fees. The commission fee for items sold online is assessed at the time when the first person requests an offline viewing. Please note that offline items that do not sell have fully refundable commission fees

Pricing Items

VetCove does not allow items to be purchased online in excess of $15,000. Any items listed for larger than that amount may only be available for offline viewing and transactions. If you are listing large valued items, please contact us directly at info@vetcove.com.
